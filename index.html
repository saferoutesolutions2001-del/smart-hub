Privacy Policy — JRM SmartRoute

Effective date: 4 July 2026  |  App: JRM SmartRoute  |  Package: com.saferoutesolutions.jrm_smartroute  |  Publisher: SafeRoute Solutions

SafeRoute Solutions ("we", "us", "our") operates the JRM SmartRoute mobile application (the "App"), used by drivers to view company-published routes, hazard alerts, authorized rest stops, and route weather guidance. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The App is provided to drivers by their employer or logistics depot for operational and safety purposes — it is not available to the general public on an open sign-up basis.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryWhat we collectWhy
Account & employment information Employee ID / username, company or depot name, and email address To authenticate you as an authorized driver for your depot (one-time-password email verification) and to associate route/hazard data with the correct depot
Location information Precise (GPS) and approximate device location, collected only while the App is open and in use To show your position on the route map, to alert you to nearby hazards and authorized rest stops, and to provide turn-by-turn route guidance
Camera Live camera feed, used only when you open the in-app QR scanner To scan QR codes (e.g. for quick destination or route lookup). Camera images are processed on-device only and are never stored, saved to your gallery, or transmitted to our servers
App preferences (stored on your device only) Depot ID, vehicle number, selected destination, language preference, and whether hazard voice alerts are enabled To remember your settings between sessions. These values are stored locally on your device and are only sent to our servers where needed to fetch the correct route/hazard data for your depot
Route & sync data The published routes, hazards, and safety guidance your depot has made available, downloaded to your device for offline use To let you view your assigned routes and safety information, including when offline

We do not collect contacts, SMS/call logs, photos from your gallery, microphone audio, or any data unrelated to the functions described above. The App contains no advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your session secure
  • To display your live position, route, and nearby hazards on the map
  • To alert you (visually and by voice) when you approach a known hazard or authorized rest stop
  • To sync the latest published routes and safety data for your depot, including for offline access
  • To provide customer support if you contact us about an issue

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Your employer / depot administrator — your employer manages driver accounts and can see operational data (e.g. route assignments, sync status) associated with your account, as part of the logistics service they use.
  • Service providers acting on our behalf — we use Mapbox for map rendering and navigation, and cloud infrastructure to host the App's backend and route data. These providers process data only to deliver the App's functionality and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements — if required to comply with a valid legal process.

4. Data Retention

Account and route/hazard data is retained for as long as your account is active with your depot, and for a reasonable period afterward for operational and record-keeping purposes. Locally stored app preferences remain on your device until you clear the App's data or uninstall it. You or your depot administrator may request deletion of your account data at any time (see Section 7).

5. Data Security

All data in transit between the App and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). Backend access is restricted by authentication and per-depot access controls, and sensitive credentials are never stored in plain text.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by employed commercial vehicle drivers and is not directed at, marketed to, or knowingly used by children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can enable or disable location and camera access at any time through your device's system settings; note that disabling location will prevent map, navigation, and hazard-alert features from working.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below, or through your depot administrator.
  • You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. The "Effective date" above will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
